Phase 5: Supervisor & Team Features Future

Live Call Monitoring Future
Supervisors see all active calls, can listen in, see real-time transcripts and AI coaching.
Whisper Mode Future
Supervisor can speak to rep without customer hearing. Coaching during live calls.
Barge In Future
Supervisor can join call as third party. Both customer and rep can hear.
Team Dashboard Future
Overview of team performance, call stats, leaderboards, and activity feed.
Rep Performance Future
Individual rep stats, call quality scores, coaching recommendations.

Phase 6: Multi-Channel Communication Future

SMS Messaging Future
Send and receive SMS via Twilio. Threaded conversations per contact.
SMS Templates Future
Pre-written templates for common messages. Quick-select during conversations.
Unified Timeline Future
See all interactions (calls, SMS, notes) in chronological timeline per contact.
Email Integration Future
Connect email accounts, send/receive emails, track opens and clicks.

Phase 7: Company Management & Settings Future

Company Settings Future
Configure company profile, Twilio credentials, callback rules, working hours.
Team Management Future
Invite team members, assign roles (admin, supervisor, rep), manage permissions.
Company Switching Future
Users in multiple companies can switch between them without logging out.
AI Agent Config Future
Configure Retell AI agents per company. Custom prompts, voices, behaviors.
Bulk Contact Import Future
Upload CSV files to import contacts in bulk. Field mapping and validation.

Implementation Notes

  • Priority: Phase 3 (Live Rep Calling) is the current focus
  • Architecture: Building in web/ folder (Next.js) - single unified platform
  • Database: All data persists in Supabase with RLS for multi-tenancy
  • Real-time: WebSocket needed for live transcription and AI coaching
  • Reference: twilio-ai-coach/ folder contains working patterns to port

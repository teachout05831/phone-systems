Website Builder Target Audience

Target Personas, Discovery Channels & Messaging Angles

Overview

Service-based companies needing website solutions, brought in through a "Grand Slam" free preview offer. Focus on segments that are easier to target, reach, and convert.

50 - 200
Target Customers per Campaign/Batch

Focus Criteria

  • Easier to target: Identifiable characteristics
  • Easier to reach: Accessible contact methods
  • Easier to convert: Motivated to change

Primary Target Segments

Segment 1: No Website

TESTING VIABILITY

Service-based businesses currently operating without a website

Pain Points

  • Missing online presence
  • Losing customers to competitors with websites
  • Don't know where to start
  • Perceive website development as expensive/complicated

Segment 2: Outdated Website

PRIMARY FOCUS

Service-based businesses with existing websites that are visibly outdated

Awareness Level: They know their website needs updating

Pain Points

  • Website doesn't reflect current business/brand
  • Poor mobile experience
  • Outdated design hurting credibility
  • Not generating leads effectively
  • Embarrassed to send prospects to their site

Business Characteristics

Business Type Service-based companies
Revenue Range Under $1M (smaller operations)
Team Size Small teams, likely owner-operated
Tech Savviness Low to moderate
Budget Mindset Cost-conscious, value-focused

Grand Slam Offer Structure

The Hook

"Free preview of your brand new website - no money required"
1
Free Website Preview
2
Free Website Build
3
Software Subscription
4
Merchant Setup

How It Works

  • Free Website Preview - They see their new site before any commitment
  • Free Website Build - If they sign up for software subscription
  • Software Subscription - Monthly recurring model (TKR Classroom platform)
  • Credit Card Merchant Setup - Integrated payment processing

Value Proposition

  • Zero upfront cost for website
  • Professional website included with software subscription
  • All-in-one solution (website + software + payments)

Qualification Criteria

Must Have

  • Service-based business
  • Either no website OR outdated website
  • Willing to use subscription software
  • Needs payment processing solution

Nice to Have

  • Under $1M revenue
  • Actively looking to grow online presence
  • Frustrated with current tech stack

Messaging Angles

For "No Website" Segment

"Your competitors have websites. Your customers expect one."
"See your business online - free preview, no commitment"
"We build it, you approve it, simple as that"

For "Outdated Website" Segment

"Your website is your first impression - is it working for you?"
"See your website transformed - free preview"
"Modern website, zero upfront cost"

Channels to Test

  • Cold email outreach
  • Social media (Facebook/LinkedIn)
  • Local business directories
  • Google Maps scraping (businesses without websites)
  • Industry-specific lists

Conversion Path

Key Insight

  • Free preview removes risk - decision becomes easy
  • They see value before committing anything
  • Subscription model provides recurring revenue

Notes

Implementation Notes

  • Web services component removed from initial offer (keep it simple)
  • Segment 1 (no website) needs validation testing before full investment
  • Focus on making the decision easy: free preview removes risk

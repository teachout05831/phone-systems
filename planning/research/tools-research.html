Outreach Tools Research

Email Finding, Outreach Automation, Social Media, Scraping & Lead Generation

Research Overview

Comprehensive research on tools for email finding, outreach automation, social media outreach, scraping, and lead generation across all channels. This covers the full stack needed for both TKOR Classroom and Website Builder outreach campaigns.

Quick Recommendations

  • All-in-One Lead + Email: Apollo.io
  • Email Deliverability: Instantly.ai (already in stack)
  • YouTube Creators: Modash
  • LinkedIn Automation: Linked Helper (budget) or Expandi (premium)
  • Local Business Scraping: Outscraper
